Fiona's Diary: Production Secrets

  • Secret #1: The "Far Far Away" kingdom was inspired by Beverly Hills and the Sunset Strip!
  • Secret #2: Puss in Boots was originally supposed to be a much larger, tough-looking cat.
  • Secret #3: The animators added "glitter tech" specifically for the Fairy Godmother’s magic.
  • Secret #4: Over 300 animators worked on Shrek 2 over the course of three years.
  • Secret #5: Crowd Technology: Significant upgrades allowed for larger crowd scenes, requiring detailed, varied costume designs for many background characters.
  • Secret #6: Setting the Scene: Production designer Doug Rogers studied real-world locations, even getting chased by an alligator while scouting a plantation for visual research
  • Secret #7: Physical Simulation: New, complex systems for hair, fur, rain, snow, and moving clouds were developed to enhance visual detail.
  • Secret #8: Advanced Rendering: The film utilized "subsurface scattering" to make skin look more realistic, along with "global illumination" to simulate natural light bouncing.
