Key Facts & Frequently Asked Questions

  • Where can I watch Shrek 2? It is available to stream on Peacock Premium and for purchase/rent on Amazon Video, Apple TV, and Fandango At Home.
  • Who are the new characters? Major additions include Puss in Boots, Fairy Godmother, Queen Lillian, King Harold, and Prince Charming.
  • What is the plot? Shrek and Fiona travel to meet her parents, who are unaware they are both ogres, resulting in tension with the Fairy Godmother, who wants her son, Prince Charming, to marry Fiona.
  • Are there any alternate versions? Yes, in some versions (notably UK), a scene where Fiona headbutts Prince Charming was replaced with a neck-chop to secure a 'U' rating.
  • John Cleese and Julie Andrews recorded most of their scenes together.
  • What is the Shrek 2 game? A 3D platformer developed by Luxoflux and published by Activision in 2004 for platforms like GameCube and PS2.
  • What are some key Shrek 2 game tips? Players can use different character abilities (e.g., Puss in Boots walks on ropes, Shrek lifts objects) and must find items like magic beans.
  • Shrek 5: The fifth installment is scheduled for release on June 30, 2027.
  • Fiona's Secret: Princess Fiona is cursed to be an ogre at night until she finds "true love's kiss".
  • Jennifer Saunders, who voiced the Fairy Godmother, performed all her own songs in the film.
  • Human Shrek was intentionally designed to resemble his voice actor, Mike Myers.

Production Secrets

  • Advanced Rendering: The film utilized "subsurface scattering" to make skin look more realistic, along with "global illumination" to simulate natural light bouncing.
  • Physical Simulation: New, complex systems for hair, fur, rain, snow, and moving clouds were developed to enhance visual detail.
  • Setting the Scene: Production designer Doug Rogers studied real-world locations, even getting chased by an alligator while scouting a plantation for visual research.
  • Crowd Technology: Significant upgrades allowed for larger crowd scenes, requiring detailed, varied costume designs for many background characters.
